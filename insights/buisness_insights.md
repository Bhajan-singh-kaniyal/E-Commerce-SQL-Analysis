# 📊 Business Insights

## 🔹 1. Revenue Insights

* Total revenue is driven by a small group of customers
* Sales show monthly variation with peak periods (likely festive season)

---

## 🔹 2. Customer Insights

* Top customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue
* Majority of customers fall into low to medium value segments
* High-value customers are key for business growth

---

## 🔹 3. Churn Insights

* Many customers become inactive after a few months
* Customers with no activity for 6+ months are likely churned

---

## 🔹 4. Product Insights

* A small number of products generate most sales
* Indicates Pareto principle (80/20 rule)

---

## 🔹 5. Geographic Insights

* Certain cities and states contribute more orders and revenue
* High-performing regions can be targeted for expansion

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Focus on retaining high-value customers
* Run targeted marketing campaigns for inactive users
* Promote best-selling products
* Expand business in high-performing regions
* Introduce loyalty programs for repeat customers

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis highlights key areas to improve revenue, customer retention, and overall business performance using data-driven decisions.
